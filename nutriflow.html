💡 Important Notes

    👋 About NutriFlow

    Hello! I'm Ransford Boah, and I created NutriFlow out of a passion for health and technology.

    Growing up in Ghana, I witnessed firsthand how nutrition impacts communities. Many people struggle with understanding what to eat and how it affects their health. That inspired me to create this platform.

    My Journey: I started as a software developer with a deep interest in healthcare. Over the years, I've worked with nutritionists, doctors, and health experts to build NutriFlow - a comprehensive platform that makes nutrition accessible to everyone.

    Mission: To democratize nutrition knowledge and help people make informed dietary choices that lead to healthier, happier lives.

    What makes NutriFlow unique:

    • ✓ 500+ organically-sourced foods from African cuisine
    • ✓ Comprehensive macronutrient and micronutrient tracking
    • ✓ AI-powered symptom-to-food recommendations
    • ✓ Doctor specialist recommendations based on health concerns
    • ✓ Easy sharing via QR codes and social media
    • ✓ Completely free and accessible to everyone

    🌍 Why This Matters

    Nutrition is the foundation of health. Yet, millions of people lack access to personalized nutrition advice. NutriFlow bridges that gap by combining technology with professional health knowledge. Whether you're looking to lose weight, build muscle, or manage a health condition, we're here to help.

    Join thousands of users making healthier choices every day!
