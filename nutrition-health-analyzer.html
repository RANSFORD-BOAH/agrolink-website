👨‍💻 About NutriHealth Analyzer

Welcome to NutriHealth Analyzer! This comprehensive nutrition and wellness platform was created to empower individuals to make informed dietary and health choices.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe that understanding nutrition is the foundation of good health. Our mission is to make nutritional information accessible, actionable, and personalized for everyone, regardless of their background or expertise level.

🌍 What Makes Us Unique

  • Local & Continental Foods: Database of 300+ foods including local African, Asian, European, American and other continental cuisines
  • Complete Nutrition Analysis: Track proteins, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, and minerals in one place
  • Symptom-Food Mapping: Intelligent system to suggest foods that help manage common health conditions
  • Doctor Directory: Connect with specialized healthcare professionals based on your needs
  • Easy Sharing: Share your nutrition plans with QR codes and social media

📊 Features Included

  • Comprehensive food nutrition database (300+ items)
  • Multi-nutrient analysis (proteins, carbs, fats, vitamins, minerals)
  • Symptom-based food recommendation engine
  • Specialized doctor recommendations
  • QR code generation for sharing
  • Social media integration
  • Mobile-responsive design

💡 How to Use

  • Nutrition Analyzer: Select foods from different categories and analyze their complete nutritional breakdown
  • Symptom Checker: Describe your symptoms and get food recommendations and doctor suggestions
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Learn about essential micronutrients and where to find them
